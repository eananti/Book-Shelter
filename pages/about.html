The Book Shelter Club

The purpose of any club is to bring a community together to learn about and discuss something that matters to them, and a book club is no different.

Our program invites small groups of ten to fifty adults to read, formulate opinions, and discuss issues related to web development from a wide array of genres.

This gives everyone the chance to discover what areas of web technologies interest them most.

Often, the thing that prevents people from learning is a misunderstanding of the basics.

The Book Shelter Club explores many types of technologies and takes into account what our students want to learn – providing them with the community necessary to talk about what they love.

Additionally, large groups and overwhelming spaces can sometimes be intimidating. Our cozy literacy studios provide the perfect escape to uncover new adventures through reading and practising.

We meet every month to learn together.

We invite top speakers to educate our learners on current trends in the tech ecosystem.
